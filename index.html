What this site does

TeamLeaders fill a survey for each engineer. The app computes axis totals and slice bias (Scenario / SOC / Pentest) from question tags, then renders a radar chart. You can export JSON, upload it later, edit marks, and re-download the chart PNG.

Recommended flow
  1. Open Survey and rate one engineer.
  2. Go to Report, download radar as PNG.
  3. Export JSON and store it.
  4. In Dashboard, import many JSONs to compare and edit later.
Scoring anchors
1 = no evidence • 3 = with guidance • 5 = independent • 7 = strong/mentors • 10 = expert/sets standards
